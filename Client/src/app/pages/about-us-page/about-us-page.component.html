About Us

Saints and Miracles was created with the intention of sharing the Catholic faith and tradition in an accessible way, especially for young people. Maintained by a small team of Brazilians, the website is written entirely in English so that this message can reach people from all over the world.

Our mission is that, in a simple and modern way, you may come to know the stories of our beloved saints, the miracles that reveal the truth of our faith and how God manifests Himself, as well as the prayers through which we communicate with Our Lord.

Here we hope to show that God is still among us, whether in the midst of wars, in remote places, or even in our daily lives, guiding history and calling each one of us to holiness.

“Do not be afraid to be saints.” — Saint John Paul II

Support This Mission

This website has no advertisements because we believe this knowledge should be free and accessible to everyone. However, keeping the site online is not free. If you would like to support this mission, you can make a symbolic donation or join our community on Discord. Every contribution helps us keep the project alive, and anything beyond the maintenance costs will be donated to the Church and to institutions connected to it.
