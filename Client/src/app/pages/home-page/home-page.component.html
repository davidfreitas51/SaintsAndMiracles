Why do we look to the Saints?

The Saints remind us that holiness is not reserved for a few, but a calling for all. They lived in different times, cultures, and struggles—yet each sought to follow Christ with courage and love.

“Be holy, for I am holy.” (1 Peter 1:16)

They were human and fallible, yet they sought righteousness. Fragile, tempted, often misunderstood, they persevered, letting God’s grace transform their weaknesses into strength.

Asking for their intercession is walking together as one family in Christ, inviting those already in Heaven to pray with us and guide us on the path they once walked.

Saints&Miracles exists to share their stories and the miracles around them, helping us learn to love God more deeply, see real people overcome weakness, and witness that God continues to act in history.

From Joan of Arc in the Hundred Years’ War to Padre Pio in World War II, Saints show us that God never abandons His people. They are friends, companions, and living witnesses of faith.
